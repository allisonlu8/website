UX Case Study

Product Overview

After going to visit your favorite bakery, you realize that it's super crowded - long line to order and no seats avaiable to sit. This website not only allows users order online and pick up at the location, but also let users reserve a table for a certain time to ensure they have guaranteed seating for whatever reason (i.e. studying, working, socializing)

Goal

Allow students and remote workers to request or reserve guarantee seating for a duration in order to efficiently do their work, thus enhancing the user experience in a busy bakery.
Our WhoBao Bakery website will let users know current available drinks/baked goods and hold the items 15 minutes in advance which will affect those that wanted a particular item and can easily pick up their items to go/to find a seat by allowing users to skip the in-store ordering and same them time. We will measure effectiveness by tracking how many users take part in this feature, and see when they start placing the orders and when they pick up the item to see if the time allocated is appropriate.
